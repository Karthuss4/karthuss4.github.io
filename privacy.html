Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

GuessThatLike! values your privacy. The app uses TikTok’s secure login (OAuth 2.0) to connect your account.

We access only limited TikTok data, such as your liked videos, as allowed by TikTok’s API and with your consent.

No personal information is sold, shared, or stored permanently. Tokens are used only during your active session.

If you have any questions, please contact: support@guessthatlike.app
